NEED A GUITARIST?

Do you need a guitar part record for your new track? All you need to do is provide a brief description of what you'd like to hear, and Alex can do the rest. The finished recordings will be sent back to you in whatever format you'd like to mix into your project. You can even use Skype to work with Alex in real-time as he is recording.

Alex has a wealth of musical experience which brings authentic and creative guitar parts to any track. Alex especially loves write and play groovy rhythms, but of course Alex will work with you to make sure everything sounds just the way you'd like.

Alex has state of the art recording equipment, a wide range of guitars, amps, microphones & signal processors to create gorgeous guitar tones. Alex understands finding the right sound and playing the part correctly is extremely important, so every effort is made to ensure that both elements complement eachother. To give you maximum flexibility when it comes to the final mix, both "wet" and "dry" versions of the guitar tracks are available.
