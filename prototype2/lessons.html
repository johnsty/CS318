LEARN. IMPROVE. PLAY

Alex is an experienced guitar tutor who has helped several guitarists get the most out the instrument.

Since he has had many different professional experiences in the music industry, Alex is able to offer real-world advice most other instructors are not able to give. No matter how skilled you are, whether your a beginner or an experienced veteran, Alex has the tools, tricks and experience to get help you you where you want to be quickly.

Alex's teaching method is based on the enjoyment of the instrument. Teaching this way is effective and leads students quickly towards improvement. Every lesson is 100% tailor made for the individuals needs, therefore there are no pre-set or generic study materials. Alex encourages all students to choose topics/styles they'd like to learn. But of course, if you are unsure of what you want to learn, Alex will be there to help you find your interest.

If there's anything else you'd like to know, feel free to get in touch with Alex here who will be more than happy to answer your questions.
